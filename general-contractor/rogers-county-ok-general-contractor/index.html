Frequently Asked Questions

What is the distinction between an estimate and a design contract?

An estimate is a free estimate that is prepared for your project. When you conclude a large remodeling, the price is for the complete work. The cost of the project may change as you progress through the design phase.

A design arrangement indicates that you have chosen our team as your builder but are unsure of the design you wish to build. The agreement is only for the design portion, so you can agree on the design and conclusive project pricing before signing a contract for the total contract sum.

How does the Home Improvement Consumer Protection Act handle emergency work?
