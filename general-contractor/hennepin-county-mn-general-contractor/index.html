What is the distinction between an estimate and a design contract?

An estimate is a free estimate that is crafted for your project. When you finish a large remodeling, the price is for the complete work. The cost of the project may change as you progress through the design phase.

A design agreement indicates that you have chosen our team as your building contractor but are unsure of the construction plan you wish to build. The agreement is only for the design aspect, so you can agree on the design and conclusive project pricing before entering into a contract for the total contract sum.

Hennepin County (/ˈhɛnəpɪn/ HEN-ə-pin) is a county in the U.S. state of Minnesota. Its county seat is Minneapolis, the state's most populous city. The county is named in honor of the 17th-century explorer Father Louis Hennepin. The county extends from Minneapolis to the suburbs and outlying cities in the western part of the county. The county's natural areas are covered with extensive woods, hills, and lakes.
