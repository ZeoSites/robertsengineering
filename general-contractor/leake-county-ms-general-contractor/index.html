Frequently Asked Questions

What is the distinction between an estimate and a design contract?

An estimate is a cost assessment that is crafted for your project. When you conclude a major refurbishment, the price is for the entire job. The cost of the project may fluctuate as you advance through the design process.

A design agreement indicates that you have chosen our team as your builder but are not certain of the design you wish to build. The agreement is exclusively for the design portion, so you can settle on the design and conclusive project pricing before signing a contract for the total contract sum.

How does the Home Improvement Consumer Protection Act handle emergency work?

Leake County is a county in the center of the U.S. state of Mississippi. As of the 2020 census, the population was 21,275. Its county seat is Carthage. The county is named for Walter Leake, the Governor of Mississippi from 1822 to 1825.
