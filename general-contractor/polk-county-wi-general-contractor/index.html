What is the distinction between an estimate and a design contract?

A pricing evaluation is a free estimate that is prepared for your project. When you conclude a major refurbishment, the price is for the complete work. The cost of the project may change as you advance through the design process.

A design arrangement indicates that you have chosen Roberts General Contractors as your builder but are not certain of the construction plan you wish to build. The agreement is exclusively for the design portion, so you can settle on the design and conclusive project pricing before signing a contract for the entire contract amount.
